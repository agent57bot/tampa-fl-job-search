### 4. Opportunity Deep-Dive Framework ⭐ **NEW**
**Template:** `opportunities/OPPORTUNITY-DEEPDIVE-TEMPLATE.md`  
**Example:** `opportunities/DEEPDIVE-Henderson-Engineers-Tampa.md`

**What This Delivers:**

For each target opportunity, a **7-part comprehensive analysis:**

1. **Company Intelligence**
   - Company overview (history, size, ownership)
   - Market position (specialization, differentiators, major clients)
   - Culture & values (Glassdoor reviews, mission, work environment)
   - Growth signals (recent news, hiring activity, market trends)

2. **Ideal Candidate Profile**
   - Job posting analysis (if available)
   - Reverse-engineered requirements (if no posting)
   - Candidate gap analysis (✅ strengths, ⚠️ partial matches, ❌ gaps with mitigation plans)
   - Fit score (0-10 scale)

3. **Network Analysis**
   - LinkedIn company page research
   - Key employees identified (Tampa office, hiring managers, HR)
   - 1st/2nd degree connection mapping
   - Warm intro paths (USF alumni, ASPE members, mutual connections)
   - Cold outreach templates (LinkedIn, email)

4. **Application Strategy**
   - Resume customization plan (specific edits for this company)
   - Keywords to mirror from job posting/website
   - Cover letter hooks (company-specific opening, why this company)
   - Portfolio/supplement materials recommendations

5. **Execution Checklist**
   - Pre-application tasks (research, materials prep, LinkedIn optimization)
   - Application submission plan (timing, method, email templates)
   - Post-application follow-up timeline:
     - **Day 1-2:** LinkedIn connections
     - **Day 10:** Email follow-up
     - **Day 14:** LinkedIn message if no response
     - **Day 21:** Second email with new info
     - **Day 28:** Decide on long-term strategy
   - Interview preparation (if advanced)

6. **Success Metrics**
   - Application outcome tracker
   - Response timeline expectations
   - Lessons learned documentation

7. **Contingency Planning**
   - What to do if ghosted after 4 weeks
   - What to do if rejected (feedback request, stay-in-touch plan)
   - What to do if interview but no offer
