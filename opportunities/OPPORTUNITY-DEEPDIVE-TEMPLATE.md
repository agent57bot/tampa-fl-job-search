### Interview Questions to Prepare

**Behavioral (Likely):**
1. "Tell me about yourself." (2-minute elevator pitch)
2. "Why are you leaving AEI after 6 months?" (Critical - prepare honest, non-negative answer)
3. "Describe a challenging project and how you overcame obstacles."
4. "Tell me about a time you had a conflict with a team member."
5. "Where do you see yourself in 5 years?"

**Technical (Likely):**
1. "Walk me through your design process for [plumbing/fire protection] on a project."
2. "How do you approach code compliance for [specific system]?"
3. "Describe your experience with Revit MEP coordination."
4. "How do you size a [fire pump / water heater / etc.]?"
5. "What NFPA codes are you most familiar with?"

**Company-Specific:**
1. "Why [Company]?" (Use research from this doc)
2. "Why healthcare engineering?" (If healthcare-focused firm)
3. "How do you handle working on multiple projects simultaneously?"

**Questions to Ask Them:**
1. "What does success look like for this role in the first 6-12 months?"
2. "How is the Tampa office structured? What's the project team dynamic?"
3. "What's the typical project lifecycle here—schematic through CA?"
4. "How does [Company] support PE licensure and professional development?"
5. "What are the biggest challenges facing the team right now?"
6. "Can you describe a recent project the team is proud of?"
7. "What's the next step in your hiring process?"
